Film Rating Predictor - Machine Learning
Pol Rosello, Jinjing Liang, Neil Parker, Michael Wang | Fall 2014

We predicted IMDB film ratings using movie features such as director, cast, budget, runtime, and genre. In particular we used a number of regression models, including Ordinary Least Squares, Lasso Regression, Ridge Regression, Logistic Regression, and Support Vector Regression. We also explored kernels and several feature selection techniques such as recursive feature selection and a custom feature scoring method.

Results indicated that Ridge Regression with a polynomial kernel performed the best, with a mean average prediction error of 0.65 on IMDB's 1-10 scale. We were also able to discover which attributes were the most influential; interestingly, runtime was the attribute most positively correlated with higher ratings.
